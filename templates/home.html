About Jovian

Jovian is an online platform primarily known for its data science and machine learning courses and bootcamps. It also acts as a job board for these fields, connecting professionals with companies in need of data science and related talent. Jovian aims to be a leading online technical university, offering a variety of resources for aspiring data scientists and software engineers.
